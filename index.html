I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Bobo Hua.

Since September 2025, I have also been a visiting PhD student at Universität Bielefeld, hosted by Prof. Alexander Grigor'yan.

My research is in geometric analysis and PDE. Specifically, I am interested in estimates and qualitative properties for elliptic and parabolic equations, including nodal geometry, hot spots conjecture, fundamental gap estimates, unique continuation, and doubly nonlinear equations.
