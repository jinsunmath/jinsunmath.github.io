I am a fourth-year direct PhD student in basic mathematics at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Bobo Hua. My research is in geometric analysis and partial differential equations.

Since September 2025, I have also been a visiting PhD student at Universität Bielefeld, hosted by Prof. Alexander Grigor'yan.

My research lies at the interface of geometric analysis and PDE. I am particularly interested in estimates and qualitative properties for elliptic and parabolic equations, including nodal geometry, hot spots phenomena, fundamental gap estimates, frequency methods, unique continuation, and long distance behavior of nonlinear diffusion-type equations.
